About Me

Well, hello! My name is Alex Wilson. I’m an aspiring web developer and this is my portfolio.

A little about myself: I grew up in Austin and moved to New York City for college when I was 18. In 2008, I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Neural Science from NYU. At the time, I wasn’t ready for any further graduate studies, so I decided to go into the family business and I started cooking. I worked in restaurants in for the next several years, trying my hand in the front of house, back of house, management. When I was ready to leave NY, I made my way down to New Orleans for a year before coming back home to Austin.

Once I was settled here, I realized I was ready for another chapter in my life. I began taking post-baccalaureate courses in physics and mathematics at the community college and then later at UT. As I worked my way through, I realized what a strong reliance these purely academic pursuits had on coding. Most of the job descriptions I stumbled upon called for a degree in physics, or computer science. So I started teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript to test the waters… And here we are! So excited to continue learning and building my body of work.

Feel free to contact me and please check out some of my past and current projects!
